Bulle civique

Following my thesis on the importance of design in political communication, I created Bulle Civique (“Civic Bubble”), an app dedicated to making politics accessible to young people. Every time a new law is passed in France, a webtoon (a short, vertical comic strip) is published on the app. The idea is not simply to illustrate the text, but to feature people directly affected by the law, so that readers can relate to the story and better understand it.
I designed a prototype of the app with Figma.
