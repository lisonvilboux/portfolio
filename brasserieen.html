La Brasserie

Group project

La Brasserie is a three-in-one space located in Rennes. It serves as a restaurant (La Cantine), a conference and meeting venue (La Halle), and office space for partner companies
(Le Studio). The brief : to design a
new visual identity for the Brasserie. We opted for colors and patterns that reflect the environments and can be easily adapted.
My role : Finalizing the logo, selecting typefaces, and designing signage.
