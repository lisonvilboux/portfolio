Le Goût
des choses

The brief: to design the visual identity and product line for a high-end chocolate brand. I’ve created Le Goût des Choses (“The Taste of Things”), a French brand that evokes nostalgia for an idealized, old-world Paris through a collage of vintage photographs.
The collection consists of three chocolate bars with floral flavors (rose, mauve, violet), creating an interesting play of colors.
